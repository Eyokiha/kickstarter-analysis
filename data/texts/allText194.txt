"Holding On" is the titled single by Ryan Layden. The music video depicts the journey a young boy will take to find his lost childhood friend. Ryan and Jessica grew up in an homeless encampment with abusive parental figures. Jessica in a desperate distraction allows Ryan to escape to a better life as she is left behind. 15 years later Ryan has returned not even knowing if she is there or even alive but is destined to find his love and rescue her.
