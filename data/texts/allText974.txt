There is a housing crisis going on within Australia and the rest of world and as a result our generation has to save for longer and take on larger debt and rent. In Australia the rental market is generally aimed at looking after the landlords pocket and generally have little standards of customer service to the tenants. Lets balance this industry by creating a trip adviser website where customers can review their landlords and create greater accountability. Risks and challenges Technology Issues: We will use a cloud based server to ensure the website is running well.
