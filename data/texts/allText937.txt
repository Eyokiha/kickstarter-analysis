WifiUnited provides members an easy way to connect to other members' WiFi worldwide, including over 1 million Open WiFi connections. Without any charges you can use your favourite social networks to stay in touch with your friends or simply browse the Internet. - No expensive roaming charges when you are abroad. - Worldwide connection not limited to national boundaries. - No more data allowance restriction from wireless or mobile provider. - - Ability for new devices to pick up Wifi settings without copying from the old device - No need to wait for an hotel Wifi password We have already completed Windows 7, 8 and 10 program for desktop and laptop computers and we have completed App for Android phones. iOS is about 85% completed. Risks and challenges The main challenge is the advertising cost and getting everybody to know about the Wifiunited service. Learn about accountability on Kickstarter
