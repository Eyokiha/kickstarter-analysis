Have you ever thought about what everyone in your social network think about you?Have you ever thought about saying something negative about someone, but afraid of talking it in front of him(her)?Well, now is the time. "What do you think about me" is the website that people can actually talk freely about people they know without showing their own identities; which means, every post will be written as anonymous.Users will sign up using their phone number or Email(possibly). So when you are looking for someone, just simply search his or her phone number. Users will have ability to show or hide the comments everyone wrote about them.Apple and Android apps are also considered during the later part of the project.
