At the beginning, Lilith was a fifteen minutes contemporary piece, create for a dance performance of the French choreographer "Gaelle Chaix". The show was ordered by the museum "Les Augustins" of Toulouse for a nocturne performance inside the beautiful room of the great Darcy stairs, in collaboration with the cultural attaché of the museum. The highly acclaimed performance was played around ten times by Gaelle Chaix and her dance company in the south of France. After some years of success, it was inconceivable to let this project stop. Due to the format imposed by the museum, I was obliged to remove a lot of ideas to shorten Lilith, to making it a finished fifteen minutes piece. Free of confines imposed by "Les Augustins", i have been able to include all the thoughts i was forced to put aside. So the new version doesn't talk only of the fall of Lilith but traces the entire story of this mythological character, from her creation to her vengeance. En Français :Au début, Lilith était une pièce de musique contemporaine de quinze minutes, créée pour un spectacle de danse de la chorégraphe française Gaelle Chaix. Commandé par le musée des Augustins de Toulouse pour une performance nocturne dans la magnifique salle de l'escalier Darcy, le spectacle est réalisé en collaboration avec l'attachée culturelle du musée. Acclamée par le publique, Lilith sera jouée une dizaine de fois dans le sud de la France par Gaelle Chaix et sa compagnie de danse. Après plusieurs années de succès, il était inconcevable de laisser ce projet s'arrêter. Au vu du format imposé par le musée, j'avais été obligé de laisser de coter un grand nombre d'idées pour raccourcir Lilith et en faire une oeuvre à part entière de seulement quinze minutes. Libéré des contraintes imposées par le musée des Augustins, j'ai enfin pu réintroduire toutes les idées que j'avais été forcé d'abandonner. Cette nouvelle version ne se contente plus de parler de la chute de Lilith mais retrace l'entière histoire du personnage mythologique, de sa création à sa vengeance.The STORY of Lilith : Lilith is a mythological character removed from the book of Genesis. First Adam's wife, she was created equal to his husband. Refusing to sexually submit to the male's domination, she was cursed and banished from the Eden. Eaten away by sadness and anger she will be coming back to take her revenge .First feminist of the history of mankind, Lilith was not a submissive woman like Eve but the equal of man. Her removing from the bible was very practical isn't it?Appart from the religious point of view, Lilith also describes the torments of a sad and angry person who is ready to destroy everything she loves to take her revenge.En Français :Lilith est un personnage mythologique retiré du livre de la Genèse. To correspond with the ambience of the museum, Lilith was created like a classical musical piece. To give a Romantique touch to the song, I used a symphonic orchestra, a choir and some classical keyboards like piano, harpsichord and organs. But, I didn't want to create a conformist piece, so I added some modern instruments : synthesizers, electric guitar and a massive rhythm section. That touch of modernity appears over the evolution of Lilith's feelings to become omnipresent when her anger and her wish of revenge override her humanity. Finally, to legitimate all those modern parts, I had decide to tell the story of Lilith in a futuristic point of view : "Once upon a time, in a future so close...Through an old radio, a mechanical priest begins his sermon:Lambs of God!
