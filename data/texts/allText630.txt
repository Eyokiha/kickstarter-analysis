CaddieMe is designed to provide caddies to all golfers! Caddies in your area will travel to local courses to provide his caddie services to customers who request them through the app. A description and rating of each caddie in the area is viewable with comments from previous golfers. A standard rate will be issued individually to each course in addition to caddies can set their rates based upon their experience and knowledge of the game. The kickstarter page will be updated daily with updates of the apps creating. Risks and challenges A challenge in creating this app is getting the approval of golf course to be listed to have caddies, not all course will accept caddies and many already have a caddie services set up.
