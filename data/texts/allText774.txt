This project is aimed at facilitating the work for, among others ,police officers, fire fighters and ambulance personnel.This smartphone application shall facilitate the work and improve the communication options between on-field working colleagues and partners by enable exact positions determination between on-duty exposed co-workers. These honorable jobs might come with serious situations. With CoFinder we want to increase the safety routines by developing a software for smartphone devices that can mark the exact 3D positions of several other app connected devices. For example; this type of software enables a firefighter to see and locate the exact position of colleagues in a burning houses.
